# Long-Term Goals

The long-term objective of this lab is to continuously develop practical cybersecurity skills while documenting architecture, design decisions, troubleshooting, and lessons learned in a manner that reflects enterprise environments.

The environment will continue evolving as new technologies are deployed, new challenges are encountered, and additional experience is gained.
